Luftbrücke nach China

SVP-Politiker auf Mission Schutzmaske

Über eine Million Schutzmasken flog die Swiss am Wochenende aus Hongkong in die Schweiz. Im Cockpit sass SVP-Nationalrat und Pilot Thomas Hurter.

Christoph Lenz

Publiziert: 29.03.2020, 21:30 Aktualisiert: 29.03.2020, 21:30

It was already dusk when the Airbus A340 touched down on the runway in Zurich at 6.05 am on Saturday morning. She had started in Hong Kong more than twelve and a half hours earlier. With empty passenger compartment but precious cargo. Around 20 tons of medical masks , suits and glasses were in the belly of the jet. Protective material that is urgently needed by hospitals, nursing homes and pharmacies in Switzerland due to the corona pandemic.

Captain on board the A340 was Swiss pilot and SVP National Councilor Thomas Hurter. The flight was very smooth, he says. The takeoff in Hong Kong made a particularly strong impression on him : “We were sitting in this empty plane shortly after midnight local time . T he cargo hold , packed with protective material and masks. Corona and a global economic crisis in the head . W hen we took off , a thunderstorm broke out – that was really special.”




Da entsteht eine Luftbrücke

Der Versorgungsflug erreicht die Schweiz zu einem kritischen Zeitpunkt: Anfang Monat waren medizinische Schutzmasken noch so knapp, dass manche Behörden dem Gesundheitspersonal empfahlen, die Masken mehrfach zu verwenden oder stattdessen schlechtere Hygienemasken zu benutzen
